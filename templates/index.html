About Our Website

At SignTalk, we are dedicated to providing a seamless video calling experience for sign language communication. The website's speech recognition technology enables individuals to understand spoken language through real-time transcription, enhancing their participation in video calls.
The intuitive design and simple controls make the website easy to navigate, ensuring a seamless communication experience for both deaf and hearing users. Our platform connects individuals using sign language, empowering them to communicate effortlessly and inclusively.

Join us in fostering an inclusive world where everyone can communicate freely, irrespective of their hearing abilities.
